Rental Terms and Conditions

1. Booking and Payment

1.1. Booking Process

To book the caravan, please contact us via the contact form on our website or by phone. We will confirm availability and provide you with a booking confirmation. A booking is considered firm once the deposit is paid.

1.2. Payment Terms and Methods

We accept bank transfers and cash payments. Full payment is due 14 days before the rental period begins. For bookings made less than 14 days in advance, full payment is due at the time of booking.

1.3. Deposit Requirements

A security deposit of â‚¬200 is required. This deposit will be refunded within 7 days after the return of the caravan, provided it is returned in good condition and all terms have been met.

1.4. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 30 days before the rental start date will receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations made 15-30 days before will forfeit 50% of the deposit. Cancellations made less than 15 days before will forfeit the entire deposit. No refund is provided for the rental fee if cancelled less than 14 days before the rental period or in case of a no-show.

2. Pickup and Return

2.1. Location

The caravan is to be picked up and returned to our location in Tartu, South Estonia. The exact address will be provided upon booking confirmation.

2.2. Times

Pickup time is typically from 14:00 on the first day of rental. Return time is by 12:00 on the last day of rental. Alternative times may be arranged subject to availability and prior agreement.

2.3. Condition Check Procedures

A joint inspection of the caravan will be conducted at pickup and return. Any existing damage will be noted. The renter is responsible for any new damage found upon return.

Late returns will incur a penalty fee of â‚¬50 per hour or part thereof, unless a later return time has been agreed upon in advance.

3.1. Permitted Uses

The caravan is to be used for recreational and holiday purposes only. It must be operated in a safe and responsible manner.

3.2. Prohibited Activities

Prohibited activities include, but are not limited to: smoking inside the caravan, transporting illegal substances, using the caravan for commercial purposes without prior written consent, and exceeding the maximum occupancy.

3.3. Geographic Restrictions

The caravan may be used within Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania. Travel to other countries requires prior written approval and may incur additional insurance costs.

3.4. Maximum Occupancy

The maximum occupancy of the caravan is 5 persons, as per its design and safety regulations.
