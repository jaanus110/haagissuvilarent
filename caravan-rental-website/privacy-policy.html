Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Caravan Rentals Tartu (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website ([Your Website URL Here, if applicable, otherwise remove placeholder]).

This policy applies to our website and services and explains your rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to enhance your experience on our website. The only personal data we actively collect and store is your preferred language setting.

Language Preference: To provide you with a seamless browsing experience in your chosen language, we store your language preference (e.g., English, Estonian, Russian) using localStorage on your device. This is an essential cookie that helps our website remember your choice for future visits.

We do not collect any other personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or location data through our website's general browsing functionality.

If you contact us directly via email or phone for booking inquiries, we will collect the information you provide (e.g., name, contact details, rental dates) solely for the purpose of processing your request and managing your booking. This information is handled separately from website browsing data and is subject to our booking terms and conditions.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect (your language preference) is used solely for the following purpose:

  • To Personalize Your Experience: Displaying the website content in your selected language.

Information provided during booking inquiries is used for communication, processing bookings, and providing rental services.

4. Data Storage and Security

Language Preference: Your language preference is stored locally in your web browser's localStorage. This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. It remains on your device until you clear your browser's cache and cookies or specifically delete it.

Booking Information: Information provided for bookings is stored securely in our internal systems with appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have several rights regarding your personal data. Since we only store language preference locally on your device via the website, these rights primarily apply to data you might provide us directly for bookings:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data that we hold.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure (right to be forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
