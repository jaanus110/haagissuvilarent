Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Caravan Rentals Tartu (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies on our website ([Your Website URL Here, if applicable, otherwise remove placeholder]). By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Types of cookies we use: We only use essential cookies on our website. These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your language preferences.

2. Essential Cookies Used

Below is a list of the essential cookies we use on our website:

  • Name: preferredLanguage
    Purpose: To remember your selected language preference so that you do not have to set it again on subsequent visits. This allows us to display the website content in your chosen language automatically.
    Storage Method: localStorage (a type of web storage that allows websites to store data locally within the user's browser).
    Duration: This data persists in your browser's localStorage until it is manually cleared by you (e.g., by clearing your browser's cache and site data). It does not have an automatic expiration date set by us.

We do not use any non-essential cookies, such as those for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

3. How to Manage Cookies

Since we only use an essential cookie stored in localStorage for language preference, traditional cookie consent banners are not required. However, you can control and/or delete localStorage data as you wish.

Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies (including localStorage) through the browser settings. You can usually find these settings in the 'options' or 'preferences' menu of your browser. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

Deleting localStorage Data: You can clear data stored by websites in localStorage by clearing your browser's cache, browsing history, or site data. The exact steps may vary depending on your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data.
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data.

Effects of Disabling/Deleting: If you delete the preferredLanguage data from your localStorage, our website will no longer remember your language preference, and you will need to select your language again on your next visit. It will not affect the core functionality of the website other than the language display.
